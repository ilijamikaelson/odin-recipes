Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a really quick and simple recipe for a tasty homemade grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Pesto Calabresey
  • Rucula
  • shredded cheddar
  • shredded gouda
  • Softended Butter

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread and butter them and spread some pesto on the insides
  2. Lay one piece on a medium hot non stick pan
  3. Top it with both cheeses and some rucula
  4. Cover with second piece of bread
  5. Flip when color is to your liking
  6. Enjoy!

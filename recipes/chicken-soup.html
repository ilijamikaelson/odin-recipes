Chicken Soup

Description

This is a really quick and simple recipe for a tasty homemade chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Cellery
  • 3 Carrots
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut all the vegetables in equal chunky pieces
  2. Place them into a large pot
  3. Place the chicken thighs on top of the vegetables
  4. Salt liberaly
  5. Cover all Ingredients with Water
  6. Bring to a boil
  7. Bring down to a simmer and cover with a lid
  8. Let it boil for 60 Minutes

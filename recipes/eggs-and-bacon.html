Chicken Soup

Description

This is a really quick and simple recipe for tasty bacon and eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 3 Slices of Thick cut Bacon
  • 1 Tbs Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives

    • Steps

    1. Cut the bacon into chunky pieces
    2. Put it in a medium hot pan and let it get crispy
    3. Crack the eggs into a Bowl and mix them with salt and pepper
    4. When the Bacon is crispy add the egg mixture and the Butter to the pan
    5. Pull the eggs to the middle of the pan with a spatula and keep cooking until slightly undercooked
    6. Plate the Eggs and garnish them with Chives
